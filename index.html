Seller FAQ

What’s in the free guide?

The guide gives you everything you need to prepare for a successful sale: a step-by-step prep checklist, a seller’s timeline, net sheet basics to understand your bottom line, disclosure insights, and our proven marketing plan. It’s designed to help you feel confident and informed before you ever hit the market.

Is there any obligation?

None at all. The guide and consultation are 100% complimentary for California homeowners. Our goal is to give you value up front — if you choose to work with us, great, but you’ll walk away with useful insights either way.

Do you work statewide?

Yes. We offer Zoom consultations for sellers anywhere in California, and in-person appointments in select regions. If we’re not the best fit, we’ll connect you with a trusted partner so you’re always taken care of.

How do appointments work?

You pick a time that fits your schedule — online or in person. Once you book, you’ll get a confirmation plus reminders by email and text. During your session, we’ll walk through pricing, prep, and your best options for moving forward.
